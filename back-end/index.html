Conscious Cook

Making the Earth cleaner one meal at a time!

Github Repository

Conscious Cook is a chrome web extension that helps you reduce your carbon footprint by adjusting your diet.

This project was made by Jainil Shah, Eric Andrechek, Mukul Agarwal, and James Han during the TitanHacks Hackathon of 2020.

Video Demo

Download

Navigate to the chrome webstore. Search up "Conscious Cook" and install the first result.

Make sure the extension is enabled in your extension settings for Google Chrome.

Navigate to the three dots on the top right side of your page.

Navigate to "More Tools" -> "Extensions". Make sure Conscious Cook is enabled.

Overview of Our Solution

We used our chrome extension to search our page for an ingredients section, and then we took in the data of each ingredient, including the amount, and passed it into our database where we search for viable alternatives for that ingredient. We created a Flask API to communicate between our backend and frontend. Our extension takes in the Carbon emission data of the ingredient and suggests a food that is similar but with lower carbon emissions so that users can try to be more mindful of where their food comes from.
